‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Will this work on my website?

Yes! The widget works on any website (HTML, WordPress, Shopify, React, Vue, etc.) as long as you can add a script tag.

What data does the chatbot access?

The chatbot queries your Google Sheet with ~311 auto parts and their prices. Product data is fetched securely from the backend server.

Can I customize the appearance?

The widget has default styling that matches most websites. For advanced customization, please contact the development team.

How do I update product information?

Simply update your Google Sheet. The chatbot checks the data every hour and always has the latest prices.

What if customers ask something outside our inventory?

The AI is trained to only answer questions about products in your inventory. For other questions, it politely redirects to contact information.
